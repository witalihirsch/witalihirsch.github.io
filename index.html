stages

Introduction and brief

We discuss the project with you - what scope of the website is needed, what problem it will solve, and other details.

Analysis

I conduct an analysis of your market, target audience, and competitors. Based on this analysis, I develop the overall structure of the website.

Prototyping

This stage helps to understand where the photo and textual content will be placed on the website and evaluate the comfort of block arrangement.

Website design style

I gather references based on your request. I search for fonts, select a color palette, and develop a cohesive overall style for the website.

Design concept

I merge the prototype with the previously approved style and create the final version of the website.

Project handover

I hand over the project to you, and the price will also include layout control to ensure that the design corresponds to reality.
